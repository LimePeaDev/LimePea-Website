🌿 Economy

  • lp!balance — Check your lime balance.
  • lp!toplimes — Leaderboard of top lime holders.
  • lp!give <@user> <amount> — Give limes to someone.
  • lp!daily — Claim your daily limes.
  • lime — Type this in reply to a lime drop to claim it!
  • lp!forcelimedrop — (Admin) Force a lime drop in the channel.

📈 Stock Market

  • lp!stock — View available stocks.
  • lp!buystock <symbol> <amount> — Buy stock shares.
  • lp!sellstock <symbol> <amount> — Sell stock shares.
  • lp!portfolio — View your stocks and their value.
  • lp!markethistory — See historical stock trends.

🧾 Profile & Customization

  • lp!profile — View your profile card.
  • lp!setdisplayname <name> — Set custom display name (max 12 chars).
  • lp!resetdisplayname — Reset display name to default.
  • lp!setbackground <id> — Equip a profile background.
  • lp!backgroundstore — Show available backgrounds.
  • lp!givebadge <user> <badge> — (Owner) Give badge.
  • lp!removebadge <user> <badge> — (Owner) Remove badge.
  • lp!listbadges <user> — List a user’s badges.
  • lp!givebackground <user> <id> — (Owner) Give background.
  • lp!removebackground <user> <id> — (Owner) Remove background.
  • lp!listbackgrounds <user> — List user's backgrounds.

📊 Leveling & Stats

  • lp!leaderboard — Leaderboard of top leveled users.

🛡️ Moderation

  • lp!warn <user> [reason] — Issue a warning.
  • lp!warnings <user ID> — Show warnings for a user.
  • lp!unwarn <user ID> — Remove a user’s most recent warning.
  • lp!kick <user> [reason] — Kick a user.
  • lp!ban <user> [reason] — Ban a user.
  • lp!unban <user ID> — Unban a user by ID.
  • lp!silence <user> <seconds> [reason] — Timeout a user.
  • lp!unsilence <user> [reason] — Remove a timeout.

🎭 Actions

  • lp!hug <user> — Hug someone.
  • lp!cuddle <user> — Cuddle someone.
  • lp!slap <user> — Slap someone.
  • lp!bite <user> — Bite someone.
  • lp!kiss <user> — Kiss someone.
  • lp!poke <user> — Poke someone.

⚙️ Utilities

  • lp!restart — (Owner) Restart the bot.
  • /embed — Create a custom embed. (Slash only)
  • lp!sync — (Admin) Sync slash commands.
